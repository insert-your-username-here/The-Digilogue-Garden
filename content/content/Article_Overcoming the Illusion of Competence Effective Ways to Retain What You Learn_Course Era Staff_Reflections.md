# How the brain stores new information
- Not all of the things stored in your short-term memory transfer to your long-term memory
## Short Term Memory 
- Short-term memory stores information you just read or learned

## Long Term Memory
 - Long-term memory works in aiding long-term retention and recall.
## Effects of the illusion of competence
- People who consistently overestimate their knowledge may have difficulty listening to any constructive criticism or negative feedback they receive, which can contribute to continued underperformance.
